About me

I am a dedicated individual with an excellent academic background in computer science and industrial engineering.My area of interest is data science including big data, statistics, multivariate data analysis and generative AI. Currently, I hold a position as a graduate research associate, where I actively contribute to cutting-edge projects and apply my knowledge to develop robust AI applications, machine learning models and also simulations of industry systems . Being from Morocco, I bring a unique perspective and a diverse range of experiences to my work. At 23 years old, I am not only passionate about my field of study but also enthusiastic about sports, including soccer, volleyball, and Formula 1. During my free time I either play soccer or drive F1 cars in simulators. Combining my technical skills and creative mindset, I strive to tackle real-world problems through innovative approaches and leverage the power of data-driven insights.
